Chapter 3

A typical Istanbulite

Overview

Bu bölümde öğrenciler, ünlü Türk yazar, mizah yazarı ve 100'den fazla kitabın yazarı Aziz Nesin'in kısa bir öyküsünü okuyacaklar. Hikaye, İngiliz bir çiftin İstanbul'u ziyaretini anlatıyor. Bir İstanbullu olan anlatıcı, çifte şehri gezdirmek için işten izin alır. Ama yaptığı turistik gezi planı işe yaramaz. İstanbul hakkında gezi kitapları okuyup ödevlerini yapmış olan çift, turist tuzaklarının ötesine geçmek ve gerçek İstanbul'u deneyimlemek ister.

In this section, students will read a short story of Aziz Nesin, the famous Turkish writer, humorist and author of more than 100 books. The story is about an English couple's visit to Istanbul. The narrator, an Istanbulite, gets permission from work to show the couple around the city. But his sightseeing plan doesn't work. Having read travel books about Istanbul and done their homework, the couple wants to go beyond the tourist traps and experience the real Istanbul.

ACTFL Proficiency Benchmarks

What I can do after working through the chapter:

I can...

  • give my own definition of Istanbulite;
  • understand a cartoon and explain what is humorous or exaggerated and why;
  • understand short opinions in a newspaper clip about the subject of being an Istanbulite;
  • state preferences, opinions, and emotions about a visit to a new place;
  • follow the main story and supporting details in a fictional text;
  • identify the author’s message and discuss it in detail;
  • describe the main characters in the text and explain how they relate to the main message of the text;
  • understand a short biographical text and summarize the main information in table format.
